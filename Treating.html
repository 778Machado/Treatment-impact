Impact of Treating Others Well

What is it? Scientific Problem Social Impact Statistics Contact Media

What is it?

Treating others well means showing kindness, empathy, and respect in daily interactions, leading to a more positive society.

Scientific Problem

Neglecting kindness can increase stress, anxiety, and social isolation. Studies show empathy boosts psychological well-being and community health.

Social Impact

Communities that promote kindness see lower crime rates, better cooperation, and improved mental health across all ages.

Statistics

  • 80% of people feel better when they do something nice for someone else.
  • Kindness reduces cortisol (the stress hormone) by 23%.
  • Schools with kindness programs saw a 30% decrease in bullying.
